Calligro Privacy Policy

Calligro is an open-source tool. We do not request or collect any personal information. All data and files provided by the user remain entirely within the user's web browser.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage (small data files stored on the user's device) for two main purposes:

  • To enhance user convenience (e.g., remembering created projects)
  • For analytics (see the Analytics section below)

Most browsers allow users to view, delete, or block cookies and local storage. Refer to your browser’s documentation for instructions on managing these settings.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to gather basic usage statistics. As a result, some analytics data is stored on Google’s servers.

We do not permit Google Analytics to use ad-related or additional tracking cookies beyond essential analytics functionality.

Policy Updates

Please note that this privacy policy may be updated at any time without prior notice.
